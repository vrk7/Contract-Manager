## üö´ Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Don't badmouth previous employers**
   - Even if experiences were negative
   - Focus on what you learned

2. **Don't say "I don't know" without follow-up**
   - Better: "I haven't worked with X specifically, but I have strong fundamentals in Y, and I learn fast - here's an example..."

3. **Don't get too technical without context**
   - Explain WHY you made decisions, not just WHAT
   - Connect to business impact

4. **Don't ask about salary/benefits in technical round**
   - Save for HR/final rounds
   - Focus on technical and cultural fit

5. **Don't be arrogant about AI/ML expertise**
   - Position as collaborative: "I can help bridge the gap between ML and product"
   - Not: "I know ML better than most"
